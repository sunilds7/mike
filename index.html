My Project idea

I plan to create a fitness application that would be useful for users ranging from beginners to highly trained athletes. This application would include workout modules, diet and nutrition tips and specific activity tracking skills. This application allows you to store your personal data, allowing you to monitor your progress overtime. Health and fitness has always been mentioned to us from a young age, however the steps and skills to attain an ideal fitness level has always been a grey area. With this application, I believe it would help elevate us individuals to a longer and healthier lifestyle. The access of this application would be one tap away however the effects of following the routine of this application would be life-changing.


According to World Health Organization (WHO), 1.4 billion adults as the study evaluates do not get enough exercise on a weekly basis, which could lead to severe health problems in the near future. This could be because many people are too busy working and can’t afford the time and money to travel to the nearest gym and spend their money on a pricy monthly subscription. Although, I am of the opinion that not all of these adults are willing to switch to a healthier lifestyle immediately, over 50% of mobile phone users in the US have downloaded at least one fitness app. Thus, I strongly believe that there is a huge untapped market for fitness applications in the world. My fitness application would be convenient for users because it would be free for the basic fitness features and provide the users the option to pay for the premium feature to further exhibit their results in the long run. The free feature would allow the user to experiment with the application and provide the user on the key insights and evaluations of the results proposed while keeping the user captivated. In comparison, my fitness application would be significantly cheaper as opposed to a personal trainer while providing equally good results. Furthermore, according to the World Health Organisation (2016), there are around 2 billion adults overweight, of those 650 million are considered to be affected by obesity (BMI ≥30 kg/m²). Some of the factors that may prevent these individuals from losing weight, include the anxiety of working out in public and the lack of understanding of the importance of a healthy diet in weight loss. My application would eliminate any form of anxiety faced from these people as they have the ability to workout from the comfort of their own homes. This could provide them more motivation to improve themselves as they are still within their comfort zones. Besides, the application also shares important diet and nutrition tips while keeping the food tasty and scrumptious. Thus, broadening the stereotype that tasty food is often unhealthy. As the popular adage goes ‘The conscious choice you make everyday lay the foundation for your mental and physical health and also determine the quality of life you lead’.


The initial feature after downloading the application is the sign up and log in. This provides the user the option to create a new personal account. The user would then have to select their targets and goals that they aim to achieve using the application. Through logging in with a personal account, it allows the application to create a more targeted and relevant fitness scheme after having specific information such as age, gender, weight and height revealed. In which allowing the application to already calculate the current Body mass index (BMI). From this calculation a mildly specific diet plan would be provided to the user having compared the BMI index to the user’s specific goals and targets. In addition, this application would allow multi-device synchronization. This feature would allow wearable products such as the IWatch and Fitbit to download this application. Thus, allowing users to constantly monitor their heart rates. Incorporating Multi-device synchronization would also permit the application to integrate geolocation allowing users to monitor their routes and planning their future routes while staying true to the original workout plan for a period of 6 months using only the base application. I strongly believe that after the period of 6 months, users would switch to the premium features as they would feel satisfied with the application and not want to lose track of all their current stored data. Not only is this beneficial for tracking your workout but this could also serve as a motivational boost. Moreover, the notification and reminder feature are essential in my application. As many people often suffer from the hustle and bustle of a busy life and tend to forget about their workout session. In such situations, the notification and reminder feature can be very useful. Lastly, the basic feature would include a tips box that allows user to share their advice on how I could make this application more appealing to them. Thus, enhancing the user experience. Having mentioned all of the basic features of the application, allow me to share the premium features for this application. Using the premium application, users would now be able to access more educational content and video tutorials. These videos may include how to develop a better mindset before and after a workout, unique techniques that should be used when approaching a more difficult workout and suggested meditation techniques for beginners. The following feature is a sleep tracker. To use this, feature the user must actively use their fitness tracker wearable for the application to monitor their heartrate and breathing rate. Next, is a water tracker. Water is as essential but neglected part of our diet. The water tracker would serve as a push notification reminder to constantly remind the user the consume it. The premium feature allows users to set the reminders as to their liking compared to the basic features which have a set time. Adding to this, in the premium feature gamification is incorporated to create more motivation and prevent the user from quitting their workout plan as the user would be rewarded with badges and rewards. Encouraging the user to work harder to win more awards in a form of healthy competition. Lastly, the premium feature allows users to share their results through integrating the application with social media. This allows the users to broadcast their amazing fitness journeys with great data and charts of their results while simultaneously advertising the application.


The tools required to create this application is Java and Oracle. Java would be the ideal software for me to create this application as I am most familiar to this programming language. Java is commonly used for banking application too. This is because Java is known to be safe, portable, maintainable and comes with better high-level concurrency tools than C++ or any other language. On the other hand, Oracle will be used to store and retrieve user’s information. High profile companies such as US cellular user oracle because its portability is rather great, superb backup and recovery while still being cost effective. Using oracle, the application would be able to store user’s confidential data and progress over an extended period of time while still maintaining its privacy.

An essential skill required to expand my business is high proficiency in Java. With a high proficiency in Java, I would be able create a better user experience, overall ensuring the user’s commitment to the application. A majority part of the programming would be dedicated to ensure that the target and goals of this user matches the training modules. The training would be listed clearly and concisely to allow the user to have a better understanding of the assigned workouts. Therefore, if my application thrives in the market, I could then hire a group of software developers to work alongside me in adding new and improved features
